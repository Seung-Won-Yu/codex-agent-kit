# Player Experience Friction Rubric

Score each area from 1 to 5. Use the score to prioritize fixes.

## Clarity

1: Player has no idea what to do.
3: Player can proceed but needs guessing.
5: Goal and next action are obvious.

## Control Feel

1: Input feels delayed, tiny, or unreliable.
3: Works but lacks confidence or feedback.
5: Tap, drag, or swipe feels immediate and intentional.

## Motivation

1: No clear reason to continue.
3: Some progress exists, but the next goal is weak.
5: Player can name what they want next.

## Reward Comprehension

1: Rewards are confusing or invisible.
3: Rewards are visible but not tied to choices.
5: Player understands what changed and why it matters.

## Pacing

1: Too slow, too dense, or too abrupt.
3: Mostly fine with one weak section.
5: Each beat arrives when the player is ready.

## Return Intent

1: No reason to come back.
3: A return hook exists but feels generic.
5: The player has a specific unfinished goal.
