# Mobile Game Review Personas

Pick one primary persona unless the user asks for comparison.

## Casual Commuter

- Has 2-5 minutes.
- Plays with one hand.
- Skips long text.
- Wants a quick win or visible progress.
- Churn trigger: slow start, unclear objective, too many menus.

## Optimizer

- Looks for efficient progression.
- Notices economy imbalance quickly.
- Wants meaningful choices and clear numbers.
- Churn trigger: shallow strategy, hidden odds, grind without mastery.

## Collector

- Motivated by characters, cosmetics, sets, rarity, completion.
- Wants preview, wishlist, collection progress, and identity.
- Churn trigger: rewards feel random and disconnected from goals.

## Competitor

- Wants fairness, ranking, readable skill expression.
- Needs fast feedback and trust in controls.
- Churn trigger: unclear hit detection, pay-to-win signals, laggy input.

## Story/World Player

- Wants atmosphere, characters, and curiosity.
- Accepts slower pacing if the fantasy is strong.
- Churn trigger: generic UI, weak stakes, too much systems language.
